Welcome To Ren And Stimpy Dot Com!!!

What is this series?

Ren and Stimpy is a comedy show created by John K. (disowned) for Nickelodeon. It was animated by Spumco (seasons 1-2) and Games Animation (seasons 3-5). The show was about the misadventures of a Chihuahua named Ren and a Manx cat named Stimpy, the plot usually revolving around different cliche themes, the duo's money-making schemes, or in random scenarios. The show also had its own parodies of advertisements, appearing before or after episodes.




Main Characters

Ren

Catchphrase: "YOU EEDIOT!"

Ren (full name: Ren HÃ¶ek) is one of the titular protagonists in the hit animated series Ren & Stimpy. He is known as a dog who is greedy, selfish, has a big fragile ego, and is very psychotic when he reaches his boiling point. He is shown to dislike his friend Stimpy, calling him slurs and harming him, but deep down he appreciates him for being a good friend. Ren was voiced by John.K (seasons 1-2), and Billy West (seasons 3-5).

Stimpy

Catchphrase: "Oh Joy!"

Stimpy (full name: Stimpson "Stimpy" J. Cat) is a comic-relief protagonist of the hit animated series "Ren and Stimpy". He is the kindest, sweetest, and most caring character in the show. He manages to endure Ren's constant berating and abuse, comforting him in times of need. However, he is very dim-witted, being interested in doing very stupid things, sticking his tongue out often, and being very terrible in decision-making. He is voiced by Billy West.





Reccuring Characters

Mr. Horse

Catchphrase: "No sir, I don't like it"

Mr. Horse is one of the reccuring characters of the show "Ren and Stimpy" He is the most frequent reccuring character in the series and is known to be overly-critical of things, taking some time to think of the incident and always responding with his signature catchphrase "No Sir, I don't like it.". He is voiced by John K. (seasons 1-2), and Billy West (seasons 3-5).

Powdered Toast Man

Catchphrase:"Leave everything to me!"

Powdered Toast Man is one of the mascots that appear in the in-universe advertisments in the show "Ren and Stimpy". He is a superhero who delivers powdered toast or rescue whenever someone calls him by his name. His superpowers consist of super-flatulence (which makes powdered toast more delicious), and flying. He was voiced by Darrin J. Sargent (season 1), and Gary Owens (season 2-5).




Ms. Buttloaves

Catchphrase: "Help! Save my (insert object here)"

Ms. Buttloaves is a reccuring character in "The Ren and Stimpy Show". She an obese woman known to play as the mother of "Old Man Hunger" and a wedlocked baby to another Lummox. She is voiced by John K (season 1-2), and Billy West (season 3-5).

Kowalski

Catchphrase:"MEAT!"

Kowalski is a big Lummox who is a reccuring character in the hit animated series "Ren and Stimpy"., He is surprisingly only 7 years old and is known to have a limited vocabulary. His temper tantrums are known to be violent, with his giant figure turning objects into dust, and he is known to also be very rude, constantly yelling and not respecting other's personal belongings. He is voiced by Harris Peet.


Muddy Mudskipper

Catchphrase: "Ya lousy bum!"

Muddy Mudskipper is a TV-star comedian who has a wife and hosts his own show in the cartoon's universe. He is known as an in-universe celebrity (who Stimpy admires and wants to meet), but behind the scenes he is sour and shows abuse to his co-stars. His depiction on the show is as a stereotypical Grizzled vaudeville comedian who stars in an afternoon kids show. He is voiced by Harris Peet (with screams and laughs done by Billy West).

Old Man Hunger

Catchphrase:"I just LOVE skinny-(insert activity here)"

A creepy balding old man who is one of the reccuring characters of the animated show "Ren and Stimpy". He has a chicken drumstick as a hat, and a small bandage covering his butt (or flowers). During the duo's mischevious adventures, he often helps them, but the help he offers isn't always helpful. He is voiced by John K. (seasons 1-2) and Billy West (seasons 2-5).
