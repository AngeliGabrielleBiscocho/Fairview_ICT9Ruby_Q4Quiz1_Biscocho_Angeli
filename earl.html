Characters
Character Description
Crisostomo Ibarra
  • Son of Don Rafael Ibarra who studied abroad
  • Came back after the news of his father's demise
  • Founded a school in San Diego, but faced huge issues in the proccess
  • Was arrested by the Guardia Civil for an attack,where he supposedly died escaping prison
Maria Clara
  • Adopted daughter of Captain Tiago, and the childhood friend of Crisostomo Ibarra
  • Sent to Colegio de Santa Catalina de Sena by her father to study
  • Was in love with Ibarra, keeping his farewell latter to prove her fidelity.
  • After learning the supposed "death" of Ibarra, was convinced by Damaso to become a nun to forget his death.
Kaptain Tiago
  • Adoptive father of Maria Clara
  • A submissive person, who cared a lot about his reputation and rank.
  • Was the one who hosted a party to celebrate Ibarra's return
  • In the end, became addicted to opium, which made Tiya Isabel leave him.
Padre Damaso
  • Former priest of San Diego
  • May be arrogant, racist, and full of himself; but he cared about Maria Clara very much
  • Was revealed to be Maria Clara's biological father.
Elias
  • Ran away from his wealthy family, along with his father and sister, after it was revealed that his father was actually the family's servant
  • After the deaths of his father and sister, became a fugitive of San Diego
  • After Crisostomo Ibarra saved his life, he repayed Ibarra by helping him in his plans
Sisa
  • Mother of Basilio and Crispin
  • Was abused by her husband
  • Went insane after both her sons went missing
Basilio
  • Eldest son of Sisa and Pedro
  • Sent to the church to work as a sacristan along with his younger brother Crispin
  • Told by Elias to cremate him and his dead mother
Crispin
  • Son of Sisa and the little brother of Basilio
  • Sent to the church to work as a sacristan along with his brother, Basilio
  • Ended up being killed after being accused of thievery
Padre Sibyla
  • A priest of Binondo and Crisostomo's former teacher
  • He is open-minded and clever and he is aware of the actions of the Spaniards
  • Would rather stay silent about the Spaniards to not get in trouble
Padre Salvi
  • The current priest of San Diego who takes his job seriously
  • Is cunning and carefully makes plans to get what he wants and at the same time, save his reputation
  • Is secretly in love with Maria Clara
Pilosopo Tasio
  • A talented person born to a rich mother
  • Coped the death of his wife and mom by drowning himself with studying about philosophy
  • Became poor due to buying many books
Don Rafael Ibarra
  • Father of Crisostomo Ibarra
  • Richest man in San Diego
  • Accidentally killed a tax collector, and was imprisoned
